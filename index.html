About Me

My name is MacKenzye Leroy, and I am a current Master of Data Science (MSDS) candidate at the University of Virginia. I've always been fascinated with finding interesting answers to complicated problems. If you do catch me inside, I'm probably cheering on my beloved New York Mets or New York Giants.
